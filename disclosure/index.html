Status labels

Owned means I have personal long-term experience with the item. Research brief means the article is based on official specifications, retailer information and community consensus rather than a claimed hands-on test. Maker profile means the piece is about context, style and buying considerations, not a guarantee about every individual knife.

Source policy

Article pages include a source trail where useful. Official maker pages, specialist retailers, technical references, KKF-style discussion and Reddit-style community consensus can all inform a piece, but the writing remains original and claims are framed carefully.

Corrections

If a specification, maker attribution, steel, heat-treatment note or retailer detail changes, the article should be corrected. Knife information moves; the site should not pretend otherwise.
