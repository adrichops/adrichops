# Content style notes

Adrichops should sound direct, practical and lightly funny without impersonating Reddit users or forum posters. Use the community style as an influence, not a template.

Preferred article discipline:

- Say whether the piece is owned, handled, researched, a maker profile or a guide.
- Include caveats and who should skip the item.
- Explain the maintenance pairing: stone, strop, board and storage where relevant.
- Use official maker/retailer sources, technical sources and community consensus carefully.
- Keep affiliate links after useful context, not before it.
- Keep articles around 7-8 minutes unless a maker spotlight genuinely needs more room.
